About This Vehicle

A rare opportunity to own the pinnacle of British motoring craftsmanship. This 2007 Bentley Continental GTC in Sandstone over Magnolia leather features the coveted Mulliner Driving Specification, adding unique diamond-quilted leather, drilled aluminum pedals, and a bespoke Mulliner clock. Powered by the legendary 6.0L W12 twin-turbo engine producing 552 horsepower, this grand tourer delivers effortless performance with a soundtrack that commands attention. The power soft-top retracts in just 25 seconds, transforming the car from grand tourer to open-air cruiser. Every surface reflects the care and maintenance befitting a vehicle of this caliber.
