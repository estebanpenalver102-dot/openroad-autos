Our Story

Two friends. One Open Road.

Danny & Andre · Founders

Brought together by a passion for cars — and entrepreneurship.

OpenRoad Auto Group was founded by two best friends, Danny and Andre, brought together by their passion for cars and entrepreneurship.

Danny, a Cuban-American, and Andre, from Brazil, met in Miami and quickly built a strong friendship based on hard work, family values, and a shared dream of creating something meaningful together.

What started as a common love for automobiles eventually became a business dedicated to helping people find the right vehicle for their lifestyle and family.
